About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. Candidate in the School of Intelligent Systems Engineering at Sun Yat-sen University. My research deeply focuses on the integration of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Cognitive Architectures. I have a profound understanding of the cognitive and reasoning mechanisms, as well as the limitations of current AI systems.


My work primarily addresses the shortcomings of existing AI frameworks in cognitive reasoning, explainability, and complex dynamic scene understanding. Through extensive development experience, I have built multiple Agent frameworks and applied Reinforcement Learning (RL) techniques, resulting in several SCI and CCF papers centered on LLMs and cognitive reasoning.
